Our Ethos

From Our Gardens to Your Cup

Chai Vana was founded on the belief that great tea should not come at the expense of people or the planet. We work directly with small, sustainable farms across Assam, Darjeeling, and Nilgiri, ensuring fair wages and ethical working conditions. Every blend is hand-crafted in small batches in Bengaluru, combining age-old Ayurvedic wisdom with modern flavor profiles.

It’s more than tea—it’s a ritual of balance, designed to bring a moment of peace to your busy, urban life.
